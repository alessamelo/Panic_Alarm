# Emergency Alarm Button

This webpage is designed as an emergency alarm button for people with medical conditions like me, who spend long hours working on a computer and may suddenly suffer a health crisis.

Its purpose is simple: with one click, it produces a loud alarm sound to immediately alert nearby people that help is needed.

In situations where speaking or moving may be difficult, this tool can help communicate danger quickly and effectively, making others aware that urgent assistance is required.

It is a simple but important way to improve safety and response during emergencies.
